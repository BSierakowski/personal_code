Welcome to the Official Website for 2008 Mid-Atlantic Women's Club Regionals Ultimate Frisbee Tournament. The tournament will be held October 4th/5th, 2008 at Pratt Park in Fredericksburg, VA.

Bids to Regionals will be awarded to each of the 3 sections in the Region (North Carolina, Capital and Founders) based up the number of teams that participate in each section's tournament.

Preliminary Seedings and Schedule are now posted (see Schedule Link). Seedings and Schedule will be finalized by Thursday October 2nd.

Games will begin at 9:00am on Saturday and Sunday.

The Tourney Fee will be approximately $300. This amount will be finalized by Thursday October 2nd. Payment is due prior to the start of play on Saturday (cash or checks made payable to Kim Kreitner).

For more information or questions contact the Mid-Atlantic Women's regional coordinator, Kim Kreitner.
